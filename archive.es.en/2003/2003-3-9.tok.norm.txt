police are appealing for witnesses to the attack which happened around the carlisle road and sackville gardens area of hove .
the assault happened on 17 february but details of the crime have only just been released .
sussex police said the 49-year-old man was asleep in his sleeping bag when the incident occurred at about 1930 gmt .
it has been described as a totally unprovoked attack but details of the man 's injuries have not been released .
police want anyone with information to contact them .
they are particularly asking joggers , cyclists and people walking their dogs along the seafront to cast their minds back to the date of the attack .
